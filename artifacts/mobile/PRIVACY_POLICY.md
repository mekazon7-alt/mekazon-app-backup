# Privacy Policy — Mekazon

**Last updated: May 2026**

Mekazon ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Mekazon mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly
- **Account information:** When you register, we collect your phone number for authentication via OTP (one-time password).
- **Home country preference:** You select your home country during onboarding. This is stored on your device.
- **Orders:** When you place an order, order details (items, quantity, delivery address) are processed through our platform.

### Information collected automatically
- **Usage data:** We collect information on how you interact with the App — screens viewed, products browsed, baskets viewed, and search terms entered. This data is used to improve the App experience.
- **Device information:** We may collect device type, operating system version, and a unique device identifier for analytics and crash reporting.
- **Location:** If you grant permission, we use your approximate location to show relevant delivery options and estimated delivery times. We do not store precise location history.

### Information we do NOT collect
- We do not collect payment card details. All payments are processed externally through our checkout partner.
- We do not collect data from children under the age of 13.
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Authenticate your account
- Personalise your shopping experience based on your home country preference
- Process and fulfil your orders
- Send you order updates and delivery notifications (with your consent)
- Improve our App through analytics
- Respond to customer support requests

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:
- **Service providers** who help us operate the App (cloud hosting, analytics, payment processing), under strict confidentiality agreements
- **Law enforcement** when required by applicable law

---

## 4. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand how users interact with the App. Firebase Analytics may collect:
- App usage events (screens viewed, items added to cart, searches)
- Device type and operating system
- Country/region (based on IP address)

This data flows into our Google Analytics (GA4) property. You can opt out by adjusting your device's advertising preferences. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy for more information.

---

## 5. Push Notifications

With your consent, we send push notifications for:
- Order confirmation and delivery updates
- Promotional offers and new product arrivals

You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your account and order data for as long as your account is active, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@mekazon.com

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

---

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the App. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
