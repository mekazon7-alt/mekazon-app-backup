## Long Description (use for both stores — max 4000 chars)

Mekazon is the home of African food in the UAE.

Whether you are from Uganda, Kenya, Ethiopia, or anywhere across the continent, Mekazon brings the tastes of home directly to your door. Shop authentic African groceries, spices, and pantry essentials — the real brands you grew up with.

**Shop what you know**
Browse Royco Mchuzi Mix, Ugali maize flour, Berbere spice blend, Ethiopian teff, and dozens more products sourced directly from African suppliers. No substitutes.

**Personalised for where you are from**
Tell us your home country and the app shows you the products, baskets, and recipes most relevant to you — whether you are Ugandan looking for Matooke and simsim, Kenyan wanting Uji mix, or Ethiopian searching for Injera flour and coffee.

**Ready-made baskets**
Save time with curated shopping baskets built around meals you already know and love. One tap, everything you need for Sunday ugali, Ethiopian coffee ceremony, or a full East African feast.

**Meal inspiration**
Discover step-by-step recipes for classic African dishes, with all ingredients available to add to your cart in seconds.

**Fast, reliable delivery**
Order before 11am for same-day delivery across Dubai and Abu Dhabi. Express 2-hour delivery available.

**Your community, your food**
Mekazon is built by Africans, for the African diaspora in the GCC. We understand what it means to miss the taste of home.
